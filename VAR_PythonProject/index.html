    GP1 - Season Table (VAR v. No VAR)

    This program displays an animated bar graph of a season table to compare scores with and without VAR intervention. The teams in green are teams that have benefitted from VAR intervention, while teams in red are teams that have worsened from VAR intervention. Hover over each image to replay the animation for the corresponding season.

    Use the dropdown menu to see stats for a specific season.

    GP2 — Team Point Evolution (VAR v. No VAR)

    This program displays an animated plot graph of a team's points over the course of five seasons since the introduction of VAR to the Premier League.

    Use the dropdown menu to see how points change for a specific team.
