GP1 - Season Table

This program displays an animated bar graph of a season table to compare scores with and without VAR intervention. The teams in green are teams that have benefitted from VAR intervention, while teams in red are teams that have worsened from VAR intervention. Hover over each image to replay the animation.

GP2 - Team Point Evolution

This program displays an animated plot graph of a team's points over the course of five seasons since the introduction of VAR to the Premier League. Select a team to show their respective graph.

GP3 - VAR Net Scores

This program displays a bar graph of participating teams for a given season. The y-axis corresponds to the number of points awarded/deducted from VAR overturns, while the x-axis corresponds to a specific team.
